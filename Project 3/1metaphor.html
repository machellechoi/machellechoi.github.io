High Fidelity: Life Rocks!

Metaphoric Poster

In the Spring 2012, I designed a metaphor poster for a book called High Fidelity. We were to come up with a metaphor relating to the book and create images that would visually represent the metaphor. My metaphor was "integrating"; pertaining to the main character's life, the book is about him growing up and finally becoming a man. For that reason, integrate, was chosen for all his life experiences coming into one, building his charcacter as a mature grown up. Therefore, I designed an abstracted image on illustrator of multiple circular lines coming into one. This particular image represents the metaphor, as coming into one, as well as a vinyl record; the grooves of the record are the lines in this image. In addition, I added some type which is the summary of the book, as well as, the title.
