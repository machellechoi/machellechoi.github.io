tagāwīt

Zine

I designed a Zine that interprets Islamic Art. To represent Islamic Art, I focused on Islamic's calligraphy and patterning. During the process, I created about 60 7x7 images through image making techniques. I used various mediums including gold flakes, spray paint, and even dried up glue from glue gun. The process was rigorous and I spent many hours on my images.
